7 I want to exchange a model I purchased, does it have to be the same branch I got it from?

You can exchange it from any Good Shoes store. In order to replace an item:

  • Please make sure that you have the original receipt,
  • The model hasn't been used, and
  • Not to exceed 14 days since the purchase date.

8 Is it possible to exchange a model I purchased Online?

You can exchange your item from your nearest Good Shoes store. You just need to have the original receipt, make sure the model hasn't been used, and not exceed 14 days since receiving the order. You can also exchange your item online, in that case you would only pay the Shipping Fees and the price difference (if there is one). If you have any inquiries regarding the Online Shop, please make sure to call our Online Shop team on the following number: 01289939695. Working hours are Sunday to Thursday from 10 AM till 6 PM.

9 What is the Return Policy?

You can return your item to your nearest Good Shoes store, just be sure to have the original receipt and that the item(s) is in the same condition as it was when you purchased it (within the 14 days).
